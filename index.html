chiken

Chicken is one of the most widely consumed and versatile poultry meats globally. Known for its tender and lean white meat, chicken is a popular protein source in a wide variety of cuisines.

beef

Beef is a popular and versatile meat sourced from cattle, primarily cows and steers. It is prized for its rich flavor and tends to be more marbled and tender compared to other meats. Beef can be prepared in various ways, from grilling and roasting to braising and ground into burgers.

sushi

Sushi is a popular and iconic Japanese dish that has gained worldwide recognition and a dedicated following. It typically consists of vinegared rice, known as "shari" or "sumeshi," combined with various ingredients such as raw or cooked seafood, vegetables, and sometimes tropical fruits.
